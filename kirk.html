Kirk Veynar

The Haunted Vessel of Seranova
Basic Info
  • Name: Kirk Veynar
  • Race: Human
  • Disorder/Condition: Splintered psyche – relives distinct ages of his life, each with its own personality, strengths, and flaws.
  • Current State: Haunted by Seranova, the god of insanity and fear.
  • Class/Role: Shapeshifter of fears — manifests different forms of terror in combat (darkness, slaughter, corruption, burial, etc.).
Timeline of Life & Split Personalities
Age 22 – The Soldier of Skyvard
Ambitious, vigorous, cruel, ruthless. Master of combat and leadership, but merciless in pursuit of victory.
Manifestation of Seranova: Captain Clay – a relentless, bloodthirsty commander.
Age 24 – The Nurse
Kind, patient, observant, but haunted by endless suffering. Empathetic healer, but spirit dimmed by despair.
Manifestation of Seranova: Dr. Therovin Whole – a cold, philosophical physician.
Age 25 – The Merchant
Practical, focused, cautious. Driven to provide for his family, but obsessed with security and fearful of loss.
Manifestation of Seranova: Mr. Pitch – a slick, smiling trader hiding daggers behind every deal.
Age 26 – The Orphanage Headmaster
Stern, intelligent, paternal, but deeply unstable. Authority and strategy, but fractured sanity and violent outbursts.
Event: Driven by madness, he maims the children under his care before relapsing into the soldier persona and fleeing. Seranova appears in multiple forms, tormenting him with fragments of all his failures.
PROPHECY
“The chained phantom walks with death’s child. Soil shall drink his body, sea shall claim his breath. To walk unburied is to carry the child’s shadow until the earth itself remembers.”
Current State – The Haunted Vessel
Now shifting uncontrollably between these personalities, Kirk fights by embodying fear itself. He manifests avatars of primal dread:

  • The Watcher – Paranoia and unseen presence.
  • The Buried – Suffocation, earth’s crushing embrace.
  • The Corruption – Rot, plague, and slow decay.
  • The Dark – Blindness, shadows, and despair.
  • The Desolation – Fury and devastation.
  • The End – Inevitability of death.
  • The Flesh – Body horror, abomination of self.
  • The Hunt – Predator instincts and relentless pursuit.
  • The Lonely – Isolation and silence.
  • The Slaughter – Frenzied, mindless violence.
  • The Spiral – Madness and illusions.
  • The Stranger – Mimicry, distrust, uncanny doubles.
  • The Vast – Fear of emptiness, the abyss.
  • The Web – Entrapment, puppetry, being controlled.
Forms of Fear
Personality Core
  • Drives: Haunted by guilt, survival instinct, manipulation by Seranova.
  • Tragedy: Each identity shows who he could have been, but Seranova twists every life path into ruin.
  • Behavior: Shifts unpredictably between warmth, cruelty, and madness.
  • Signature Trait: In combat, his form shifts to reflect the fear he wants his enemy to feel.
