Circle of the Maddening Deep

“Fear is older than the gods, deeper than the roots of the world, and written into the marrow of every living thing. Those of the Maddening Deep do not flee from fear—they become it.”

Level 2 — Aspect of Dread
You learn to channel ancient fear as part of your Wild Shape.

Dreadform Wildshape. When you use Wild Shape, you may assume one of the Fear Forms from the Gyrmm’s Guide to Maddening Forms instead of a beast. These forms reflect ancient fears (such as The Buried, The Dark, The End, etc.). You gain additional forms as you gain levels, as detailed in your Fear Forms document.

Level 6 — Dream-Eaten Memory

Echo of Terror (1/rest). As an action, choose one creature you can see within 60 feet. They must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC = 8 + PB + Wisdom) or be haunted by one of your Fear Forms for 1 minute. While haunted:

  • They have disadvantage on saving throws against being frightened.
  • Once per turn, when they take damage, they must make a Wisdom saving throw or take 1d8 psychic damage.
  • The creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.

Mark of Fear. While in a Fear Form, your strikes are considered magical for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity. If a creature is frightened of you, your attacks against it deal additional psychic damage equal to your Wisdom modifier (once per turn).

Level 10 — Mantle of Dread

Frighten the Heart. When you assume a Fear Form, you may immediately force all creatures of your choice within 30 feet to make a Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute (save ends each round). You may use this feature once per short or long rest.

Level 14 — Avatar of Fear

Terror Made Flesh. You can maintain your Fear Form for an indefinite duration and can cast druid spells while transformed, ignoring verbal and somatic components.

Form of True Horror (1/day). As a bonus action, you assume a Nightmare Form for 1 minute. While in this form:

  • Your size becomes Large, and your AC equals your Druid level + your Wisdom modifier.
  • You gain resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
  • Creatures frightened by you have their speed halved.
  • You can cast druid spells while in this form, ignoring verbal and somatic components.

STR 16 (+3) • DEX 8 (-1) • CON 18 (+4) • INT — • WIS — • CHA —

Senses: Tremorsense 30 ft. (60 ft. at DL 10+), passive Perception 10.

Traits

  • Suffocating Grip. While grappling a creature, use a bonus action to deal 1d4 bludgeoning damage (scales with DL).
