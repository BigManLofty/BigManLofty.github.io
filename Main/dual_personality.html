The Soldier
Believes he is a soldier. His personality is very cruel, but he is always happy.
  • Speaks with authority and harshness
  • Enjoys discipline and order
  • Finds joy in conflict and challenge
  • Shows little empathy, but is cheerful
  • Quick to make tough decisions
The Doctor
Believes he is a doctor. He is very kind, but records everything he sees.
  • Gentle and compassionate in speech
  • Always seeks to help and heal others
  • Meticulously observes and documents details
  • Prefers peace and understanding
  • Maintains a journal of all encounters
Doctor's Notes:
"Every interaction, every wound, every word is worth recording. Observation is the first step to healing."
The Orphan Caretaker
A caretaker of orphans who loves violence and is very creepy.
  • Speaks in a soft, unsettling tone
  • Finds joy in chaos and conflict among children
  • Encourages fights and watches with delight
  • Always smiling, but the smile never reaches their eyes
  • Has a collection of disturbing stories and objects
Caretaker's Whisper:
"Children, don't be afraid of the dark. It's where the real fun begins... and I do love to see you play rough."
