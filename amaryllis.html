Amaryllis Alward

High Elf • Cleric of Valiki Laz • Champion of Light and Night

Amaryllis Alward’s life began far beneath the shining towers of the upper city. Born to the Kulvir family in the undercity, her childhood was marked by grit, survival, and love in the shadows. But fate struck cruelly when raiders tore her family apart, leaving her orphaned and alone.

The Alwards — a noble house of scholars and clerics — took her in. Though she fought against it at first, trying to escape back into the streets she once called home, Amaryllis found herself bound to this new life. Slowly, the roots of her past and present began to intertwine.

Chosen by the Twice-Faced Star

At the age of fifteen, Amaryllis’s life changed forever. Her goddess appeared. Valiki Laz, the Twice-Faced Star — radiant and haunting, dressed in carnival finery with a mask split between sun and moon.

When Valiki spoke her name, Amaryllis stepped forward. The goddess bent to meet her at eye level and placed a blessing on her. In that moment, Amaryllis accepted her calling: to become a cleric, a vessel of both light and night.

“The stars do not question why they shine. Neither should you.” – Valiki Laz

Forged in Faith and Fire

At sixteen, Amaryllis entered the Summorum Academy, where she devoted herself to divine rhetoric, clerical study, and the mastery of both prayer and blade. Though she began behind her peers, her determination — and the quiet guidance of her goddess — pushed her to the top of her class.

She became a scholar, a healer, and a champion of faith. But she never forgot the undercity, nor the pain that shaped her. In her heart lives a fire: the belief that justice must come to those who deal in cruelty.

The Alward Family

  • Althea (Mother) – High Cleric of Valiki Laz. Strict, but kind. A healer and councilwoman.
  • Desmond (Father) – Sorcerer, professor, a man of intellect and legacy.
  • Callidora (Sister, 27) – Brilliant, beautiful, and “perfect.” Both a guide and a source of pressure.
  • Ciro (Brother, 16) – Bright and ambitious, eager to forge his own path at the Academy.

Though not her blood, the Alwards are now her family — and through them, she found belonging she once thought impossible.

Today

At twenty-two, Amaryllis Alward walks with her goddess’s blessing and her family’s name. She is engaged to Dimitri Savard, carrying hope for love into the next chapter of her life.

But beneath her poise and grace, she is still that girl from the undercity — determined, unyielding, and unwilling to let injustice go unchallenged.

“My goddess will have her justice.” – Amaryllis

Symbols and Colors

Domains of Valiki Laz: Light • Night

Symbol: A theatrical mask split between sun and moon.

Colors: Light blue, sage green, pale pink, white, and gold.

Timeline

Years 1–10: The Undercity Child
Amaryllis was born into the Kulvir family, deep in the undercity. Life there was harsh but filled with love, resourcefulness, and resilience. These years forged her toughness and independence.

Year 10: The Raid and the Loss
Tragedy struck when raiders attacked the Kulvir family’s hostel. Amaryllis’s parents and sister were killed, and she was taken to the upper city. Orphaned, she was adopted into the noble Alward household — strangers in a world of sunlight and privilege.

Years 11–15: A Stranger in the Sunlight
These were years of adjustment and conflict. Surrounded by kindness yet weighed down by pity, Amaryllis struggled to accept her place among the Alwards. During this time, she met Valiki Laz, the Twice-Faced Star. Blessed by her goddess, Amaryllis accepted her divine calling.

Years 16–21: Scholar of the Academy
Amaryllis was accepted into the prestigious Summorum Academy. She studied divine rhetoric, clerical magic, history, etiquette, and arcane theory. Though she began behind her peers, her relentless determination and her goddess’s blessing helped her rise to the top of her class.

Year 21: The Promise of Love
At twenty-one, Amaryllis met Dimitri Savard. Their bond grew quickly, and within six months, they were engaged. For the first time, Amaryllis allowed herself to hope for love and a future built on more than duty.

Year 22: The Present Flame
Now, at twenty-two, Amaryllis stands at a crossroads. She is a daughter of the Alwards, a champion of Valiki Laz, and a cleric caught between light and shadow. Her story is still unfolding — her flame burns brighter than ever.

The Legend of Amaryllis

From the ashes of loss to the heights of divine power, Amaryllis Alward stands as both spark and storm. She is proof that no origin, no sorrow, and no shadow can hold back the light.
